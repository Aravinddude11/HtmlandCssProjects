The Man who rules the Search Engine.

Pichai speaking at the 2015 Mobile World Congress in Barcelona, Spain

Here's a time line of Pichai Sundararajan life:

  • 1972 - Sundar Pichai was born on July 12 in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India.
  • 1993 - Pichai earned his Bachelor of Technology degree in Metallurgical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kharagpur.
  • 1995 - He completed his Master of Science in Material Sciences and Engineering from Stanford University.
  • 2002 - Sundar Pichai earned an MBA from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.
  • 2004 - Pichai joined Google and started working on the Google Toolbar.
  • 2008 - He played a significant role in the development of the Chrome browser and the Chrome operating system.
  • 2012 - Sundar Pichai became the Senior Vice President of Chrome and Apps, overseeing products like Chrome, Chrome OS, and Google Drive.
  • 2013 - Pichai took over Android, Google's mobile operating system, in addition to his responsibilities with Chrome.
  • 2015 - Sundar Pichai was appointed CEO of Google on August 10, 2015, and Google became a subsidiary of Alphabet Inc.
  • 2019 - Pichai became the CEO of Alphabet Inc., succeeding Larry Page.
  • 2022 - Pichai received the Padma Bhushan in the category of Trade and Industry from the Government of India, the country's third-highest civilian award.
